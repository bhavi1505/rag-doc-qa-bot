A business strategy is a plan that outlines how a company will achieve its goals and compete in the market. It involves analyzing market conditions, identifying opportunities, and making decisions about resource allocation.

Key components of a business strategy include value proposition, target market, and competitive advantage.

Companies use strategies such as cost leadership, differentiation, and focus to gain an edge over competitors.

Technology plays a major role in modern business strategies, especially with the use of data analytics and artificial intelligence.

A well-defined strategy helps organizations grow, adapt to changes, and achieve long-term success.
